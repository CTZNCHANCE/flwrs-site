The Platform

A Cultural Recognition Segment & IRL Experience

FLWRS is an intimate, long-form conversation series that serves as a cultural recognition platform — honoring pioneers, builders, and visionaries for actively shaping culture across music, sports, media, business, and social impact.

Incubated and featured on platforms including Sway in the Morning on SiriusXM — one of the most trusted voices in culture — FLWRS brings together worlds that are usually separated by industry lines, in a way that feels earned, human, and lasting.

The segment extends beyond the broadcast into IRL events, conferences, and pop-up experiences — creating moments of recognition that live across audio, video, social, and physical space.

WHY
FLWRS

  • Cultural impact is often celebrated too late — after the moment has passed, after the builder has moved on, or worse
  • Audiences are seeking meaning and authenticity in a media landscape flooded with disposable content
  • Brands want cultural alignment, not interruption — they want to stand beside culture, not in front of it

FLWRS meets this moment by spotlighting people whose work moves culture forward — in real time, within a trusted and influential media environment.

Segment
Format

FLWRS features monthly on highly engaged, culturally relevant platforms and IRL events — including live on Sway in the Morning, broadcast from the SiriusXM Los Angeles studio.

Each installment centers on a long-form, in-studio conversation focused on impact, leadership, and legacy — with extended life across every touchpoint.
